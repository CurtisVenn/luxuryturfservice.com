Good Questions

FAQ

The things homeowners want to know before they commit — answered plainly.

What exactly is top dressing? +

Top dressing is applying a thin, even layer of premium material — sand or a sand/organic blend — across the lawn surface and working it down into the canopy. It levels minor unevenness, improves how water moves through the soil, and creates a healthier environment for turf roots and density over time.

Will it help with pooling water or dry spots? +

In many cases, yes. A sand-based top dressing improves infiltration and reduces surface pooling caused by minor compaction or grade issues. We'll tell you honestly upfront if a problem is beyond what top dressing can solve — so you're never paying for the wrong fix.

When is the best time for aeration + overseeding in Utah? +

Timing depends on your grass type and the weather cycle, but Utah's best windows are typically early fall for cool-season grasses. Aeration + top dressing + overseeding together is one of the fastest paths to a noticeably thicker lawn. We'll recommend the right window during your quote.

Is it safe for my kids and pets? +

We offer organic options and a soil-first approach that prioritizes long-term health over shortcuts. If you have specific concerns — pets with sensitivities, kids who play on the lawn — mention it in the quote request and we'll tailor the blend accordingly.

Do you post pricing online? +

Not yet — because properties vary significantly in lot size, slope, access, and what services make sense. We keep the quote process simple and fast: fill out the form, we come back to you with a clean, specific number. No fluff, no upsells.
